RegTech

ARGOS

AML Compliance & Regulatory Technology Platform

A RegTech platform addressing the critical AML compliance gap in Panama's banking sector and broader emerging markets. ARGOS delivers automated regulatory intelligence, transaction monitoring, and reporting tools — bridging the gap between evolving compliance requirements and operational capacity in the region's financial institutions.

  • AML transaction monitoring & screening
  • Regulatory reporting automation
  • Panamanian banking sector focus
  • Scalable to broader LAC compliance markets
